Our major causes

What is the use of living, if it be not to strive for noble causes… Winston Churchill

All-round Exposure

Focusing on the holistic development of the students, we acquaint them with the various aspects of science, medicine, humanities, law, business, entrepreneurship, etc. and assist them in choosing a career option suitable to their choices and interests.

Placements & Jobs

We assist the graduates and post-graduates in finding a suitable job by connecting them with a web of employers and guide the civil-services aspirants about the various stages of examinations.

General Awareness

With an aim to ensure the better upbringing and learning of children, we regularly conduct the parents and teachers training sessions. We also conduct various activities to aware the masses about general issues.

We are nonprofit team

MAUR EDUCATION WELFARE SOCIETY is a nonprofit organization which presently operates in and around the district of Bathinda in Punjab. The Society, through its diligent efforts, aims to contribute in the promotion of education and skill development and incessantly works for the same. Envisaging the role of today’s students as the leaders of future, the Society specifically works towards enhancing their leadership qualities by providing them a platform and the scads of such related opportunities.

The Society is also sincere towards its social obligations and continuously works for the same by helping the unprivileged children in their education and by organizing various educational programmes for the specially-abled students.
